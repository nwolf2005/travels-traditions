The Show

Over Two Decades on Public Television

Travels & Traditions is the longest-running and highest-rated travel series on PBS. For more than 25 years, the show has told stories about the places, people, and traditions that define how we experience the world.

Founded by James Beard Award-winning journalist Burt Wolf and now produced by his son Nicholas, the series has aired nearly 100,000 verified times across 168 markets nationwide.
